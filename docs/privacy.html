Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

1. Introduction

MathPaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the MathPaster Chrome Extension.

2. Data Collection & Usage

To provide you with premium features, MathPaster collects limited information necessary for account management and payment processing.

  • Personally Identifiable Information: We collect your email address when you create an account to manage your premium subscription.
  • Authentication Information: We collect authentication data (such as login credentials or OAuth tokens) to securely log you into your account and verify your premium status.
  • Payment Processing: All financial transactions are securely processed by Stripe, our third-party payment provider. We do not directly collect, store, or process your credit card numbers on our servers.
  • No Tracking of Content: We do not track your behavior or the equations you type. All mathematical processing happens locally on your device.

3. Permissions Explained

MathPaster requests specific browser permissions solely to function as a productivity tool:

  • activeTab and Host Permissions: These are required to inject the WYSIWYG editor into the webpage you are currently viewing and to insert your generated LaTeX math into text input fields.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, any updates will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
